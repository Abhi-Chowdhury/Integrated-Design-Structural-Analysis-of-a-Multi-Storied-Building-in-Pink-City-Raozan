As a final year student of B.Sc. in Civil Engineering and as a part of the CE - 412 course I along with my team mate designed a residential building in Pink City, Raozan, Chittagong. 
Here we prepared the required plan in the Autocad software according to BNBC 2020.
Then we designed the building in ETABS software. 
After that we analyzed the structure, did a serviceability check and analyzed the building in Etabs. 
At last we prepared Bill of Quantity (BoQ) for the respective building.
